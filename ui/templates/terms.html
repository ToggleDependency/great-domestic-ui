Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement explains how we use the information you submit to us, including any personal information, in relation to the Directory. We value your trust and your privacy is important to us, so we will keep your information secure and manage it in accordance with our legal responsibilities. By providing us with information, you agree to it being used and disclosed in the manner set out in this Statement.

What information do we collect and what do we use it for?

We collect information we need in order to consider your application for pre-registration on the Directory. Ultimately, we may use this same information to create an entry relating to your business on the Directory:

  • Company name;
  • Company address;
  • Contact name;
  • Email address;
  • Telephone number;
  • Website;
  • Preferred contact channel;
  • Companies house number;
  • Sector
  • Product or service offered;
  • Description of product or service.

[Whilst you are not required to provide all of this information to us, the more information you provide, the more successful your entry on the Directory is likely to be.]

You understand that, if your application for pre-registration is successful, the information above will be included on a publicly accessible platform.

We also ask you for some additional information. This information is not for inclusion on the Directory, but will be used by trained professionals within our organisation to research and analyse the behaviour and make-up of exporting businesses, in order to produce relevant reports. Any reports produced will only include aggregated data, from which it will not be possible to identify individuals or individual businesses. The additional information we request is as follows:

  • Exporting experience;
  • Frequency of exports;
  • Turnover accountable to exports;
  • Destinations to which you export;
  • Range of export destinations.

Who else do we provide your information to?

In determining your application for pre-registration, your information may be reviewed by a number of members within our team, and by members of other UK Government departments.

[Creation of the Directory requires assistance from third party service providers, for example hosting services and developers, and we may need to disclose your information to these third parties.]

Upon launch, the Directory will be a publicly accessible platform. Therefore, if an entry relating to your business is included, your information will be freely accessible on that basis.

You may opt out of these additional communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link included in our emails, or by writing to us at the address set out below.
