Get finance

You can get finance to export from government through UK Export Finance (UKEF). UKEF is the export credit agency for the UK. It helps UK companies to:

  • win export contracts by providing attractive financing terms to their buyers
  • fulfil contracts by supporting working capital loans
  • get paid by insuring against buyer default

Complete a short enquiry form to get free, impartial guidance from an export finance manager. Select ‘Get finance’ from the enquiry options. You’ll be asked about your exporting experience, where you work and how you want to be contacted.

Find out more about UK Export Finance.
