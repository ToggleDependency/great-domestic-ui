Market research

Research is an essential first step to selling to a new overseas market. Making decisions based on market data will help you maximise profits and avoid costly mistakes.

Articles:Market research

Customer insight

Defining who your customers are is vital. Begin with what you know about your current customers. It’s likely there will be similarities between them and potential customers in a new market.

Articles:Customer insight

Finance

Export finance can provide the working capital you need to be competitive in overseas markets, help you get paid and insure against non-payment.

Articles:Finance

Business planning

It’s important to use data to make an informed decision on how to enter a market. The way you choose to sell and market your product or service will affect your profits and success in that country.

Articles:Business planning

Getting paid

Getting paid for international sales isn’t always straightforward. You should think about which currency to invoice in, at what point you get paid and how the money will reach you.

Articles:Getting paid

Operations and compliance

To be successful at selling overseas you need to get your products where they need to be and get them there on time. You'll need to decide whether to manage logistics yourself or to use a freight forwarder.
