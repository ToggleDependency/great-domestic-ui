About

Great.gov.uk brings together international trade and investment information and services from across government. It is maintained by the Department for International Trade (DIT).

DIT is the government department responsible for promoting and financing international trade and investment, and championing free trade.

What's on great.gov.uk

You can:

  • read guidance to help you export your product or service
  • get market statistics and guidance to match your experience
  • create a profile to showcase your business to overseas buyers
  • find an online marketplace to sell your products overseas
  • apply for opportunities to sell your product or service overseas
  • get finance advice and support from UK Export Finance
  • find events and trade missions to attend

Overseas companies can:

  • read guidance on how to invest in the UK
  • find a UK supplier
  • see Visit Britain for information about visiting the UK
  • see the British Council website for information about studying in the UK
