Trade barriers are measures that slow down or prevent a UK business from accessing an overseas market. Reporting a trade barrier will help the UK government to understand the issues that businesses are facing so we can provide support where it's needed most.

Trade barriers, also known as non-tariff barriers, can affect businesses of all sizes at different stages in the exporting process. The government can support by understanding what barriers are affecting you. Find out more information and examples of about a trade barrier on GOV.UK or submit a report.

How it works

You can submit a report to UK government with this service. If your barrier is an emergency, for example, your goods are held at border, you should call this number [00 000 0000] or view our emergency contacts for urgent support. You can still submit a report so we are aware.

Making a report online

  2. Describe the problem

    What is the barrier that you've experienced? Is the barrier still affecting your business?
